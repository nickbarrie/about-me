Crash Recovery

Crash Recovery is an adventure game implemented in Java, representing a significant milestone in my programming journey. With a robust architecture consisting of over 40 Java classes, this project served as a pivotal learning experience, allowing me to tackle the complexities of a larger-scale program. Throughout its development, I encountered numerous challenges and invaluable lessons, enriching my understanding of software design and implementation.

In Crash Recovery, players embark on a journey through various levels, tasked with defending their character against formidable enemies. The ultimate goal? To gather essential ship parts scattered across the terrain, crucial for repairing the protagonist's vessel following a crash landing. This adventure not only showcased my technical skills but also fostered creativity and problem-solving abilities, shaping me into a more proficient developer.
